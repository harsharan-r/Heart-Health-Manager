    Please select the symptoms you or someone around you is experiencing:

    Balance

    The person is experiencing dizziness and/or is unable to balance themselves

    Eyes

    Cannot see in one or both of their eyes.

    Face

    Face appears to be uneven.

    Arms

    Arms are weak/numb, person is unable to lift their arm(s).

    Speech

    Person's speech is slurred, they are having trouble speaking, seem confused.

    Shortness of Breathe

    Difficulty in breathing, person seems exhausted and is having a shortness of breathe.
